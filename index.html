About Us

Engineering excellence since 2004

Arkax Engineering

Arkax Engineering, Inc. is a highway and bridge construction company that specializes in complex major infrastructure projects. Founded in 2004, the company has grown from humble beginnings into one of the region leading major highway and infrastructure construction firms, with active Design-Build contracts valued at over $1.9 billion.

The company credits its successes to the many dedicated, talented and hard-working engineers and field personnel that have made the company what it is today. The underlying philosophy is that a company is only as good as the people it employs, and every team member is considered an important part of its continued success.

Today, Arkax Engineering has the expertise, labor and equipment to self-perform all major work items including earthwork, drainage, roadway base, asphalt paving, major and minor bridges, concrete paving, retaining walls, and concrete and steel piling. The company owns over 400 pieces of equipment and operates its own asphalt and precast concrete production facilities.

Company History
2004
Arkax Engineering is founded. First project awarded: a $2.1M drainage contract on a major arterial highway.
2007
The company establishes its Bridge Division, winning a contract including three bridges over a major canal.
2010
First project valued over $50 million awarded. Equipment fleet expands to over 150 pieces.
2013
Joint venture wins a $124M viaduct project - completed 120 days ahead of schedule. Multiple national awards.
2016
Arkax expands into asphalt production, establishing The Arkax Asphalt Group for vertical integration.
2019
Wins the $599M Gateway Interchange Design-Build project. Design innovations save the DOT $45M.
2022
Prestress-Precast Division launched, supplying I-Beams, MSE wall panels, and precast piling.
2024
Arkax Engineering reaches $1.9B in active contracts, becoming one of the region top infrastructure firms.
