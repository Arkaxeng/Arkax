Services

Engineering support from analysis to practical execution

Services

Our engineering services are built around one goal — making sure every project is structurally sound, constructible, and delivered with confidence. From early design through final execution, we bring technical clarity to the decisions that matter most, helping owners, contractors, and developers move forward without uncertainty.

Analysis / Structural Design

We provide structural analysis and design services for residential and commercial buildings. Our team evaluates load paths, floor and roof systems, column and wall capacities, and foundation conditions to deliver designs that meet building code requirements and perform reliably under real-world conditions.

About Us

Structured protection through precise engineering

ARKAX Engineering

ARKAX Engineering was officially established on April 30th, 2026, but its foundation is built on more than 20 years of structural engineering experience delivering solutions across a wide range of complex projects.

The name ARKAX is inspired by the concept of the Ark as a symbol of protection, resilience, and structure under extreme conditions. It represents the idea of engineering systems designed to safeguard what matters, even under uncertainty. The "AX" introduces precision, direction, and decisiveness — reflecting the way we approach engineering challenges.

Together, ARKAX represents structured protection through precise engineering.
